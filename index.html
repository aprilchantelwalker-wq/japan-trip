📅 How to use the Itinerary
  • Collapse/expand days by tapping the day header, or whole cities by tapping the city bar.
  • Filter by couple using the All / Together / C&A / P&J buttons at the top.
  • Add something with the ＋ button — new items start as 💡 Idea so everyone can weigh in.
  • Vote on ideas using ✅ In / 💭 / 👋 Skip — when both couples hit ✅ In it shows 🎉 Both In!
  • Together + Confirmed items (flights, hotels) have no vote row — those are locked in.
  • Drag the ⠿ handle to reorder items within a day.
🗺 How to use Discover
  • Browse 102 spots from our Google Map — filtered by city and category.
  • Tap 📍 Map on any card to pan the map to that location.
  • Tap ✅ Mark done during the trip to track what you've visited.
  • Toggle ☰ List / ⊞ Grid view with the buttons top-right.
  • Tap ＋ City to add a new city if your plans change.

🎫 How to use Bookings
  • All flights, hotels and activities to book are listed here.
  • Status: ✅ Confirmed = done · ⏳ Pending = in progress · 📌 Book Now = needs booking · 🕐 Line Up = no reservation, just show up.
  • Tap ✏️ to edit any item — you can add a booking link or confirmation number in the Notes field.
  • Tap ＋ in any section header or the ＋ FAB bottom-right to add a new booking.

🚄 How to use Transit
  • All Shinkansen and train routes for the trip are listed here.
  • Tap 📌 Book on Klook ↗ on any unbooked route to go straight to the booking page.
  • Once booked, edit the card (✏️) and update the status to Confirmed + add seat numbers.
  • Transit Tips section has Suica card, luggage delivery, and eSIM info.
💡 How to use Tips
  • Tap any section header to expand or collapse it.
  • Covers: apps to download, helpful links, transport tips, etiquette, phrases, and cherry blossom forecasts.
  • Tap ＋ on any section to add your own tip. Drag ⠿ to reorder.
  • Japanese Phrases — tap to edit, English on the left, Japanese on the right.
🎵 How to use TikToks
  • All Japan TikToks saved by the group, sorted by category.
  • Tap ▼ Preview to watch inline, or Open ↗ to open in TikTok.
  • Tap 🤍 to like — your couple's name appears as a badge. Filter by ❤️ Liked to see your saves.
  • Add a 💬 comment on any video — both couples can discuss it here.
  • Tap ＋ Add at the top to save a new TikTok link.

💬 How to use Notes
  • Real-time shared board — anything posted here is visible to both couples instantly.
  • Pick your couple at the top, choose a type (💡 Idea, 🗳 Vote, 📝 Note…), and hit Post →.
  • React to notes with 👍 ❤️ 🔥 ✅ — tap again to remove your reaction.
  • You can 🗑 delete your own notes using the trash icon.
  • The 🔔 bell in the top-right shows activity from the other couple.

🛍 How to use the Wishlist
  • Four private lists — one each for April, Connor, Julia and Patrick.
  • C&A can only see April's and Connor's lists. P&J can only see Julia's and Patrick's.
  • Toggle between the two names in your couple using the buttons at the top.
  • Tap ✓ on an item when you buy it — it gets a strikethrough and the progress bar updates.
  • Tap 📤 on any item to share it as a 💌 suggestion for the other couple.
  • Tap ＋ (FAB or store header) to add items. You can set the store, size/price notes, and whether to share.
